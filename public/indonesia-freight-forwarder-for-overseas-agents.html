FAQ

Indonesia freight forwarder for overseas agents FAQ

Can overseas freight forwarders work with Ambara in Indonesia?

Yes. Overseas freight forwarders can work with Ambara for Indonesia-side coordination, including air freight, customs clearance support, undername import support, DDP/DDU coordination, airport cargo handling, final delivery, and local shipment updates.

What does an Indonesia destination agent do?

An Indonesia destination agent helps coordinate local shipment handling, document review, consignee or importer follow-up, customs clearance coordination, cargo release, delivery, and shipment updates after cargo moves toward Indonesia.

Does Ambara support air freight shipments for overseas agents?

Yes. Ambara supports air freight import and export shipments for overseas agents, including pre-alert review, AWB and document checking, airport cargo handling, and shipment status coordination.

Can Ambara coordinate customs clearance in Indonesia?

Ambara can coordinate customs clearance support based on shipment requirements, commodity type, documents, importer or exporter readiness, and applicable Indonesian regulations.

Can Ambara help if the consignee does not have importer readiness?

Ambara may help coordinate undername import support when importer readiness is not available, subject to commodity, documents, HS code, regulatory requirements, and risk review. It is not a universal solution for all goods.

Does Ambara provide DDP or DDU delivery support in Indonesia?

Ambara can review and coordinate DDP or DDU delivery support depending on service scope, tax and customs arrangement, cargo profile, required charge funding, and delivery destination.

Can Ambara handle cargo outside Jakarta?

Ambara can coordinate delivery and local handling across Indonesia depending on cargo profile, route, delivery address, and local handling requirements.

What documents should overseas agents send before shipping to Indonesia?

Overseas agents should send the commercial invoice, packing list, AWB draft if available, commodity description, HS code if available, gross weight, dimensions, number of packages, origin, destination, party details, service scope, special handling requirements, target date, and required delivery SLA if any.

Does Ambara support one-time and recurring shipments?

Yes. Ambara can support one-time shipments and recurring shipment flows, subject to shipment details, service scope, documents, cargo profile, and operational feasibility.
