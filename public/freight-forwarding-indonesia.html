FAQ

Freight forwarding Indonesia questions

What does a freight forwarder do in Indonesia?

A freight forwarder coordinates cargo movement, documentation, customs-related support, cargo handling, and delivery between origin and destination.

Does freight forwarding include customs clearance?

Freight forwarding may include customs clearance coordination depending on the agreed service scope. Customs-related steps depend on the commodity, documents, importer/exporter readiness, and applicable regulations.

Can Ambara support overseas freight forwarders?

Yes. Ambara supports overseas freight forwarders and logistics agents that need Indonesia-side cargo handling, customs coordination, delivery support, and local shipment follow-up.

Can a foreign company ship to Indonesia without a local importer?

In many commercial import cases, an importer or consignee setup is required in Indonesia. If the consignee does not have the required import setup, Ambara may help coordinate undername import support depending on the commodity, documents, and regulatory requirements.

Does Ambara provide DDP shipping to Indonesia?

Ambara can support DDP shipment coordination depending on the commodity, route, importer arrangement, customs requirements, tax considerations, and delivery location.

Does Ambara provide sea freight?

Ambara's core strength is air freight. Selected sea freight or multimodal shipment coordination may be available through appointed partners when required.
