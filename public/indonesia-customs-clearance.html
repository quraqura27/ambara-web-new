Common problems

Why Indonesia customs can slow down cargo

These issues do not always stop a shipment, but they can create questions, inspections, storage costs, or extra document work if they are discovered late.

Document mismatch

Commercial invoice, packing list, AWB, shipper, consignee, quantity, weight, and cargo descriptions do not match.

Unclear HS code or product description

The cargo description is too broad, the HS code is uncertain, or product details are not enough for pre-shipment review.

Consignee or importer readiness

The local consignee may not have the right information, tax details, authorization, or supporting documents ready for the process.

Regulated cargo questions

Some goods may require additional review, permits, certificates, or authority coordination. Ambara Artha can assist with checking and preparation, but approvals are not guaranteed.

FAQ

Indonesia customs clearance questions

Can Ambara Artha guarantee customs clearance in Indonesia?

No. Customs clearance depends on cargo details, documents, consignee readiness, applicable regulations, inspection outcomes, and government decisions. We can assist with document review, preparation, coordination, and risk reduction.

Can you check if my cargo may need special permits?

We can review the cargo information, flag potential permit or restricted-goods questions, and coordinate the next confirmation steps. Approval depends on the relevant authority.

Can you support cargo arriving at CGK?

Yes. Ambara Artha can support CGK cargo coordination, document follow-up, local communication, and trucking or delivery coordination where applicable.

When should we contact you?

Contact us before cargo departs when possible. Early review helps identify missing documents, consignee readiness issues, or regulatory questions before the shipment is already in transit.
