A New Force in Indonesian Air Freight

PT Ambara Artha Globaltrans officially launched in 2025 with a singular mission: to make international air freight forwarding simple, transparent, and reliable for Indonesian businesses of all sizes.

Built on Decades of Expertise

While we are a new company, we are built on extraordinary experience. Our management team and advisory board bring over 40 years of combined expertise in air freight cargo operations. We know the airlines, the routes, the regulations, and the relationships that make global logistics work.

Our Home: CGK Cargo Terminal

Based at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport Cargo Terminal, we have direct access to all major international airlines operating from Indonesia. Our strategic location means your cargo can move on the next available flight — not the next available day.

What We Offer

From standard air freight and express next-flight-out (NFO) services to cold chain logistics, customs clearance, and sourcing & procurement support, Ambara Artha provides a complete suite of air cargo solutions tailored for Indonesian exporters and importers.

Track Your Cargo Online

Our real-time tracking platform lets you follow your shipment from CGK to its final destination. Enter your tracking number at ambaraartha.com to get started.
