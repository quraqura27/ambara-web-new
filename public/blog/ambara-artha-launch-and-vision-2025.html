Our Story

PT Ambara Artha Globaltrans was founded with a single mission: to be Indonesia's most reliable and transparent air freight partner for businesses worldwide. Based at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport's cargo terminal in Jakarta, we combine 40+ years of industry expertise with modern digital tools to deliver a freight experience that is fast, visible, and stress-free.

Why We Started

Indonesia is one of the world's great trading nations, yet many foreign importers and exporters struggle with opacity, communication barriers, and unreliable service when shipping to and from Indonesia. We started Ambara Artha to change that — providing English-speaking, digitally-enabled freight forwarding from the heart of Indonesia's largest cargo hub.

What We Offer

  • Full-service air freight forwarding from CGK, SUB, and DPS
  • Licensed PPJK customs clearance
  • Real-time shipment tracking
  • 24/7 operations team
  • Bilingual service (English and Indonesian)
  • Transparent, all-inclusive pricing

Our Target Markets

We serve businesses and individuals worldwide who need to import or export goods to/from Indonesia, including importers of Indonesian handicrafts, textiles, and food products; exporters shipping to Indonesia's growing consumer market; e-commerce businesses fulfilling orders from Indonesian suppliers; and multinational companies managing Indonesian supply chains.

Our Commitment

Every shipment we handle is treated with the same care and urgency as if it were our own. We communicate proactively, resolve problems quickly, and always give you honest answers — even when the news isn't what you hoped to hear.
