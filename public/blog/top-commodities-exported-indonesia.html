Indonesia as a Global Exporter

Indonesia is the world's 4th most populous country and a major global supplier of natural resources, manufactured goods, and agricultural products. Air freight plays a crucial role for high-value and time-sensitive exports.

1. Electronics and Components

Indonesia is a major manufacturer of consumer electronics, smartphone components, and computer peripherals for global brands. These high-value items are typically shipped by air due to their value-to-weight ratio.

2. Textiles and Garments

Indonesia is one of the world's top textile exporters. Fast-fashion brands source from Indonesian factories and rely on air freight for urgent replenishment orders.

3. Fresh Seafood and Aquaculture

Indonesian shrimp, lobster, tuna, and other seafood are exported fresh by air to Japan, Singapore, USA, and Europe. Cold chain logistics are critical.

4. Tropical Fruits and Vegetables

Mangosteen, salak, dragon fruit, and other exotic Indonesian fruits are exported by air to Asian and Middle Eastern markets.

5. Handicrafts and Furniture

Bali and Yogyakarta are world-famous for handmade furniture, wooden crafts, silver jewelry, and batik textiles. These are primarily exported through DPS and CGK airports.

6. Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices

Indonesia has a growing pharmaceutical manufacturing sector. Temperature-sensitive medicines require air freight with cold chain handling.

7. Rubber Products

Processed rubber products, gloves, and industrial components are exported by air for automotive and healthcare supply chains.

8. Processed Food and Snacks

Indonesian instant noodles, snacks, and specialty foods have huge global markets especially among the Indonesian diaspora in Singapore, Malaysia, Netherlands, and Australia.

9. Cosmetics and Herbal Products

Indonesian natural beauty products, jamu (herbal medicine), and cosmetics are growing export categories shipped by air to global markets.

10. Automotive Parts

Indonesia is a regional automotive manufacturing hub. Spare parts and components are frequently air-freighted for just-in-time supply chains.
