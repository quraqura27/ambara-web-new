Customs & Regulations

PPJK (Pengusaha Pengurusan Jasa Kepabeanan) is an official Indonesian Customs Clearance Agent license issued by DJBC (Directorate General of Customs and Excise). Having a licensed PPJK means we can legally process customs declarations on your behalf, ensuring compliance and faster clearance.
Prohibited items include: narcotics, weapons, counterfeit goods, protected wildlife (CITES), and items banned by Indonesian law. Restricted items (requiring special permits) include: food products, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and electronics. Contact us to verify your specific commodity.
Yes. Import duties into Indonesia are based on HS Code, CIF value, and commodity type. Rates vary from 0% to 40%+ depending on the goods. Our team will calculate estimated duties during the quoting process and guide you through PIB (Import Declaration) filing.

Pricing

Chargeable weight is the higher of actual weight (kg) vs volumetric weight. Volumetric weight = (Length × Width × Height in cm) ÷ 6000. For example, a 100×100×100 cm box = 166.67 kg volumetric. If actual weight is 50 kg, you're charged for 166.67 kg.
Key factors: chargeable weight, origin/destination route, commodity type (general, perishable, DG), service level (standard vs express), fuel surcharges, security surcharges, and handling fees. We provide transparent all-inclusive quotes with no hidden costs.
