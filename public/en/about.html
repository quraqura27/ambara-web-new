Our Story

Built on Decades of Freight Expertise

PT Ambara Artha Globaltrans was founded in 2025, but our roots run deep — our leadership team brings over 40 years of combined experience in Indonesian air freight, customs brokerage, and international logistics.

Strategically positioned at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport's cargo terminal, we have direct access to all major airlines and cargo facilities, enabling faster processing and competitive rates for our clients.

Our mission is simple: be the most reliable and transparent freight partner for businesses shipping to and from Indonesia.
